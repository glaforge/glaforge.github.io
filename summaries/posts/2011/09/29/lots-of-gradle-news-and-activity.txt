Dokter emphasizes three core areas of enhancement: the introduction of a new dependency cache, the implementation of a build daemon, and general performance improvements across the system. In this piece, Dokter critically examines a pervasive issue faced by other build systems: the problem of non-reproducible builds. He attributes this problem primarily to inadequacies and poor design in their existing dependency cache solutions. He then proceeds to explain in detail how Gradle's newly designed dependency cache directly addresses and elegantly resolves these critical issues, aiming to ensure consistent and reproducible builds for Gradle users.
