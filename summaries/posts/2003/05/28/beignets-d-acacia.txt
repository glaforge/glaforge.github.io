**The Recipe for Acacia Flower Fritters:**

*   **Preparation Time:** 15 minutes
*   **Ingredients (for 4 gourmands):**
    *   500 g of flour
    *   2 eggs
    *   2 glasses of milk
    *   1 pinch of salt
    *   2 tablespoons of sugar
    *   Acacia flowers (a medium-sized salad bowl full)

*   **Preparation Instructions:**
    1.  In a large bowl, combine the flour, eggs, salt, sugar, and milk, mixing them together to form a smooth batter.
    2.  Carefully remove the individual flowers from their clusters (égrainer les fleurs), rinse them lightly under water, and then drain them thoroughly.
    3.  Add the prepared acacia flowers to the batter and mix them in evenly.
    4.  In a deep fryer (or a large pot), heat oil (or "végétaline") until it is boiling hot.
    5.  Using a spoon, carefully drop spoonfuls of the flower and batter mixture into the hot oil.
    6.  Once the fritters are beautifully golden brown, remove them from the oil using a slotted spoon or skimmer.
    7.  Lightly sprinkle the hot fritters with sugar and serve them fresh.
