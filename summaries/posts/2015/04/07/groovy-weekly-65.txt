**Key Infrastructure Changes and Apache Incubation:**
The most critical update is the ongoing transition of the Groovy project into incubation at the Apache Software Foundation. This strategic move entails the migration of core infrastructure components. Specifically, the official JIRA issue tracker for Groovy has successfully moved to Apache's infrastructure, and the Groovy mailing lists are also in the process of relocating to new addresses under the Apache umbrella. This signifies a major step in the project's governance and community structure.
