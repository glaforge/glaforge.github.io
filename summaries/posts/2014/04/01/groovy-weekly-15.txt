**Key Releases:**
The period saw several notable software releases. **Spring Boot 1.0 GA (General Availability)** was announced, highlighted by its robust Groovy support, indicating a growing synergy between the two technologies. **Ratpack 0.9.3**, a reactive web framework, also received an update. **Glide 0.3.2** was released, integrating the latest **Gaelyk 2.1.1** and **Google App Engine 1.9.1**. The **GVM SDK**, a tool for managing Groovy environments, saw a new version deployed. **SimpleCI**, a continuous integration server built entirely in Groovy, achieved stability and moved to a rolling release cycle, signifying its maturity. Additionally, Sergei Egorov introduced his **MacroGroovy** project, designed to simplify the creation of AST (Abstract Syntax Tree) transformations in Groovy.
