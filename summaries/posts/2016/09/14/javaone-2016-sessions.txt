This article serves as a personal preview and announcement by the author regarding their upcoming attendance and participation at JavaOne, an annual gathering for Java developers held in San Francisco. The author notes that this will be their tenth time attending the event.

The core of the article outlines the author's speaking engagements and highlights sessions from their Google colleagues, as well as a significant number of talks related to the Groovy ecosystem.

**The Author's Sessions:**

The author is slated to participate in two distinct sessions:

1.  **"Java and the Commoditization of Machine Intelligence" (CON2291):** This is described as a panel discussion that will feature representatives from major tech companies, including IBM, Microsoft, and Google. The discussion will revolve around Machine Learning APIs. The author's specific role on the panel will be to cover the Machine Learning APIs offered by Google Cloud Platform, specifically mentioning Vision, Speech, and Natural Language APIs.
2.  **"A Groovy Journey in Open Source" (CON5932):** In this session, the author plans to provide a historical overview of the Apache Groovy project. Beyond its origins, the talk will also cover the latest developments and new features within the Groovy language and its ecosystem.
